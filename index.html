NAYI SUBAH FOUNDATION

"A New Dawn of Hope"

We are an alliance of passionate, driven students hailing from Lahore Grammar School (LGS JT Johar Town). Having actively spearheaded impactful youth initiatives before—including mobilizing competitive community networks like the CyberClash Tournaments—our foundational mission has scaled.

❤️ Our Core Mission Motive

"The definitive purpose of the Nayi Subah Foundation is to serve and protect the underprivileged, mobilizing direct community aid and resources to uplift the poor and support orphans across our society."

🤝 Why Join Nayi Subah?

Nayi Subah is founded on the conviction that the youth of Lahore must drive change under structured student leadership. By stepping up, you obtain direct real-world execution experience in helping orphans and the poor while mastering:

  • ✨ Grassroots Welfare Deployment
  • ✨ Orphanage Support Logistics Management
  • ✨ Strategic Social Impact Fundraising
